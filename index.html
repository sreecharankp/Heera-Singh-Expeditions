About Me

My name is Heera Singh Bisht. I am a trek leader from WAN, Uttarkhand . My trekking includes Roopkund, Brahmatal and Chandrashila.

Currently running my own Trekking Organization ( #TrekWithHeera ). Previously worked as a trek leader/guide in Indian Hikes and Trek The Himalayas . Having 10+ years of trekking experience, I have completed Roopkund trek atleast 60 times.

Currently having a group of 3 people to support me. (Indira Singh Bisht, Krishna Singh Bisht, Alam Singh Bisht and Mules to carry our luggages).

FAQs

It depends on the season and the region you visit. You may probably get to see snowfall during December, January, April, June.

I have seen small kids (~8 years) to age old people (65+ years) completing the trek. It depends on the mentality, the interest & the motivation.

Physical fitness is a must. Usually I suggest the people to go for running for 5KM daily for 30+ days. Try to complete 5KM in 20-25 minutes. Initially it will be difficult, but you will pick up gradually.

If you are planning to do trekking every now & then, then I strongly suggest you to buy the equipments. Otherwise go for the rental option. Here is cost of some of the equipments
  • Trekking shoes: Rs 300/- per trek
  • Trek pant: Rs 300/- per trek
  • Jacket: Rs 300/- per trek
  • Trekking pole: Rs 150/- per trek
  • Poncho: Rs 100/- per trek
P.S: Price of the equipments might vary depending on the availability.

Food, tea, snacks etc will be served during trekking. Everything is included in the trekking cost. You may bring some chocolates.

That depends. Please contact me to discuss further.
